Theo Serlin

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at Stanford University. My research is in international and comparative political economy. I am especially interested in how spatial processes&mdashmigration and agglomeration&mdashinfluence people's preferences over policies and reactions to economic change.

I graduated from Harvard College in 2018 with an AB, summa cum laude, in History. I received an MA in Economics from Stanford in 2022.
